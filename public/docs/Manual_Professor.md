# AFSMS v1.0 User Manual - Professor Role

## 1. Overview
Professors are responsible for evaluating student performance and maintaining academic integrity within their assigned disciplines.

## 2. Grade Entry
- Navigate to 'Grades'.
- Select your discipline and group.
- Input grades (1-10) or mark as 'Absent' (0).
- Click 'Finalize' to commit the grades to the official record.

## 3. Document Management
- Upload course materials and syllabi.
- Review student requests assigned to you.
