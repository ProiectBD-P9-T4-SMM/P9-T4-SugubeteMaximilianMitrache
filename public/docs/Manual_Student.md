# AFSMS v1.0 User Manual - Student Role

## 1. Overview
Students have read-only access to their academic progress and can submit official requests to the secretariat.

## 2. Academic Progress
- **Dashboard**: View your current GPA and ECTS credits.
- **Grades**: Check your results for the current and past semesters.

## 3. Document Workflow
- Submit requests for certificates, transcripts, or other documents.
- If a document is returned for modification, you will see a notification and can re-upload the corrected version.
